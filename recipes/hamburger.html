Hamburger

Delicious Hamburger with cheese and bacon

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling.
This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and no tasteless bread crumbs!
Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Patty
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Ingredients

  1. Grill Patty and bacon
  2. Toast Bread
  3. spread butter
  4. add patty
  5. add cheese
  6. add bacon
  7. shovel into your face hole
